Whether it's a family visit, celebrating a birthday, or giving Alex a break from the kids. Waco is Texas' best kept secret and memory maker!

Let this webpage be your guide as you explore this beautiful stop in between Dallas and Austin.

Must See

Magnolia Silos

Lets face it, this is the reason y'all came. This is by far the most magical place in Waco. With so many shop, food trucks, and sweet treats the Silos is perfect for an all day excursion. More

Fabled Bookshop and Cafe

Do you love book or love the idea of owning many leather bond volumes of classic literature? Then this is the place for you. Fabled has it all, including literally inspired beverages for adults and kids.More

Heritage Homestead

Step back in time and see how people lived before TikTok. With farm fresh food and handmade goods, the Heritage is an excellent place to shop and eat. Also it might be a cult..not sure. More

Mayborn Museum

Located in the Baylor University complex, this museum is the perfect way to spend an afternoon. Explore the natural history of Waco and visit the state of the art DC Comic exhibit going on now! More
